Frequently asked questions

How do I report a bug or unexpected behaviour?

Please email us at the support email address above and include a brief description of what happened and the steps to reproduce it. Screenshots are always helpful. It's best to indicate what kind of device you were using (e.g. tablet or phone, ios or android). You can also directly open an issue on GitHub

Which devices and iOS versions are supported?

Sootja runs on ios and Android devices. Please check your app store for minimum version requirements.

How do I request a new feature?

We welcome feedback. Send your idea to our support email and we will take it into consideration for future updates.
